# Pokémon Trading Card Game Pocket — Expansions

> A structured inventory of PTCGP expansions and their notable cards, grouped by series and set, designed to be easy to parse and reuse. For what cards are (kinds, types, anatomy, rarity), see [`overview.md`](./overview.md) §2; for battle mechanics, see [`game-rule.md`](./game-rule.md).

**Document status:** Research compiled mid-2026 from Bulbapedia, Serebii, Limitless TCG (pocket.limitlesstcg.com), Game8, and official sources. Card totals, codes, and dates are cross-confirmed and reliable; per-set "secret"/star-tier lists run to dozens of cards each and are summarized to the notable/chase cards rather than enumerated exhaustively. The newest sets (B3a, B3b) and the upcoming B4 are the least settled. Source URLs are at the end.

**Conventions used in this document**

- **Set code** uses the in-game series notation (A1, A1a, … B1, B1a, …). Mini-sets carry a letter suffix.
- **Card terminology** — energy types, card kinds, the rarity ladder, and pull rates — is defined in [`overview.md`](./overview.md) §2 ("Cards"). This document uses the rarity symbols **◇** Diamond, **☆** Star, **✸** Shiny, **♛** Crown as shorthand.

## 1. A Series (2024 – 2025) — Generations I–VII era

### Genetic Apex — A1 (最強の遺伝子)

- **Released:** October 30, 2024 (launch set) · **Cards:** 286 · **Theme:** Kanto / original 151
- **Booster packs (3):** Charizard pack · Mewtwo pack · Pikachu pack (some cards are pack-exclusive)
- **ex cards (◇◇◇◇):** Venusaur ex, Charizard ex, Blastoise ex, Pikachu ex, Mewtwo ex, Gengar ex, Machamp ex, Marowak ex, Arcanine ex, Exeggutor ex, Starmie ex, Articuno ex, Zapdos ex, Moltres ex
  - _Pikachu pack notables:_ Pikachu ex, Blastoise ex, Arcanine ex, Zapdos ex
  - _Charizard pack notables:_ Charizard ex, Exeggutor ex, Moltres ex, Starmie ex
  - _Mewtwo pack notables:_ Mewtwo ex, Venusaur ex, Gengar ex
- **Immersive (☆☆☆):** Charizard, Mewtwo, Pikachu, and **Mew** (Mew is awarded for completing the "Complete the Kanto Pokédex!" mission — collecting the first 150 dex cards)
- **Crown (♛):** Charizard, Mewtwo, Pikachu (gold Crown Rares)
- **Meta note:** The foundational set; Mewtwo ex, Pikachu ex, and Charizard ex defined the launch metagame.

### Mythical Island — A1a (幻のいる島)

- **Released:** December 17, 2024 · **Cards:** 86 · First "mini" set · **Theme:** Mew / mythical
- **Booster pack (1):** Mythical Island (mascot: **Mew**)
- **ex cards:** Celebi ex, Gyarados ex, Mew ex, Aerodactyl ex, Pidgeot ex
- **Crown (♛):** Mew ex (#086) — sole Crown Rare
- **Notable secrets (☆/☆☆):** alt-art Exeggutor, Serperior, Salandit, Vaporeon, Dedenne, Marshadow; Supporters **Blue** and **Leaf**
- **Gimmick:** Mew is hidden in the artwork of many cards (Joltik, Stonjourner, Weezing, Chatot, etc.).

### Space-Time Smackdown — A2 (時空の激闘)

- **Released:** January 30, 2025 · **Cards:** 207 · **Theme:** Sinnoh (Gen IV)
- **Booster packs (2):** Dialga pack · Palkia pack
- **ex cards:** Dialga ex, Palkia ex, Darkrai ex, Gallade ex, Weavile ex, Infernape ex, Yanmega ex, Pachirisu ex, Mismagius ex, Lickilicky ex
- **New mechanic:** first **Pokémon Tool** cards — Giant Cape, Rocky Helmet, Lum Berry
- **Supporters:** Cynthia, Cyrus, Volkner, Dawn, Mars
- **Meta note:** Dialga ex and Darkrai ex were major meta cards. _(The A2 update also introduced the Trading feature — see [`overview.md`](./overview.md) §5.)_

### Triumphant Light — A2a (超克の光)

- **Released:** February 28, 2025 · **Cards:** 96 · Mini set · **Theme:** Arceus
- **Booster pack (1):** Triumphant Light (mascot: **Arceus**)
- **ex cards:** Arceus ex, Garchomp ex, Leafeon ex, Glaceon ex, Probopass ex
- **Crown (♛):** Arceus ex (#96)
- **Mechanic:** "Link" abilities that synergize when Arceus / Arceus ex is in play
- **Supporters:** Irida, Barry, Adaman, Celestic Town Elder

### Shining Revelry — A2b (シャイニングハイ)

- **Released:** March 27, 2025 · **Cards:** 111 (72 base + 39 secret) · Mini set
- **Significance:** **Introduced the Shiny rarity tiers (✸ / ✸✸)** and the **first Gen IX (Paldea) cards**
- **Booster pack (1):** Shining Revelry (headline: Shiny Charizard ex, Pikachu ex)
- **ex cards (9 — one per type except Dragon):** Beedrill ex (Grass, #003), Charizard ex (Fire, #010), Wugtrio ex (Water, #019), Pikachu ex (Lightning, #022), Giratina ex (Psychic, #035), Lucario ex (Fighting, #043), Paldean Clodsire ex (Darkness, #048), Tinkaton ex (Metal, #054), Bibarel ex (Colorless, #065)
- **1-Star (☆) full-arts (#73–78):** Meowscarada, Buizel, Tatsugiri, Grafaiai, Gholdengo, Wigglytuff
- **2-Star (☆☆) Supporters:** Iono (#088), Pokémon Center Lady (#089), Red (#090), Team Rocket Grunt (#091)
- **Immersive (☆☆☆):** Giratina ex (#096)
- **Shiny 1-Shiny (✸, #97–106):** Weedle, Kakuna, Charmander, Charmeleon, Wiglett, Dondozo, Pachirisu, Riolu, Varoom, Revavroom
- **Shiny 2-Shiny (✸✸, #107–110):** Beedrill ex, Charizard ex, Wugtrio ex, Lucario ex
- **Crown (♛):** Poké Ball (#111) — gold Item card
- **Outside set numbering:** Shiny Mewtwo ex (paid-bundle exclusive, no rarity symbol)

### Celestial Guardians — A3 (双天の守護者)

- **Released:** April 30, 2025 · **Cards:** 239 (155 base + 84 secret) · **Theme:** Alola (Gen VII)
- **Booster packs (2):** Solgaleo pack (mascot Solgaleo ex, Metal, #122) · Lunala pack (mascot Lunala ex, Psychic, #87)
- **ex cards (10):** Decidueye ex (Grass), Dhelmise ex (Grass), Incineroar ex (Fire), Crabominable ex (Water), Wishiwashi ex (Water), Alolan Raichu ex (Electric), Lunala ex (Psychic), Passimian ex (Fighting), Alolan Muk ex (Darkness), Solgaleo ex (Metal)
- **Immersive (☆☆☆):** **Guzma** (#208) and **Lillie** (#209) — the game's **first immersive Trainer/Supporter cards**
- **Full-art Supporters (☆☆):** Acerola, Ilima, Kiawe, Guzma, Lana, Sophocles, Mallow, Lillie
- **Shiny (✸/✸✸):** returning Kanto lines reissued as shinies — Bulbasaur→Venusaur ex, Squirtle→Blastoise ex, Jigglypuff→Wigglytuff ex, Gastly→Gengar ex, Machop→Machamp ex, Cubone→Marowak ex, Exeggcute→Exeggutor ex, Staryu→Starmie ex (non-ex = ✸, ex = ✸✸)
- **Crown (♛):** Lunala ex (#238), Solgaleo ex (#239)

### Extradimensional Crisis — A3a (異次元クライシス)

- **Released:** May 29, 2025 · **Cards:** 103 (69 base + 34 secret) · **Theme:** Ultra Beasts (introduces the "Ultra Beast" label)
- **Booster pack (1):** Extradimensional Crisis (cover: **Buzzwole**)
- **ex cards (5):** Buzzwole ex (Grass, 140 HP, #6), Tapu Koko ex (Lightning, 130 HP, #19), Lycanroc ex (Fighting, 150 HP, #33), Guzzlord ex (Darkness, 170 HP, #43), Alolan Dugtrio ex (Metal, 130 HP, #47)
- **1-Star (☆, #70–75):** Rowlet, Pheromosa, Blacephalon, Alolan Meowth, Silvally, Celesteela
- **Full-art Supporters (☆☆):** Gladion (#81), Looker (#82), Lusamine (#83)
- **Immersive (☆☆☆):** Buzzwole ex (#88)
- **Shiny (✸, #89–98):** Growlithe, Arcanine, Froakie, Frogadier, Greninja, Jynx, Pidgey, Pidgeotto, Pidgeot, Aerodactyl
- **Shiny (✸✸, #99–102):** Celebi ex, Arcanine ex, Aerodactyl ex, Pidgeot ex
- **Crown (♛):** Nihilego (Darkness, #103) — a non-ex Crown
- **Ultra Beasts in set:** Nihilego, Buzzwole, Pheromosa, Xurkitree, Celesteela, Kartana, Guzzlord, Poipole, Naganadel, Stakataka, Blacephalon

### Eevee Grove — A3b (イーブイガーデン)

- **Released:** June 26, 2025 · **Cards:** 107 (69 base + 38 secret) · **Theme:** Eevee & Eeveelutions (art by PLANETA)
- **Booster pack (1):** Eevee Grove
- **ex cards:** Flareon ex, Primarina ex, Sylveon ex, Dragonite ex, Snorlax ex
- **1-Star (☆) full-arts:** the Eeveelutions (Vaporeon, Jolteon, Flareon, Espeon, Umbreon, Leafeon, Glaceon, Sylveon)
- **Shiny (✸):** Pinsir, Lapras, Voltorb, Electrode; shiny ex of the legendary birds and Gallade ex
- **Supporters:** Hau, Penny · **Tool:** Leftovers
- **Crown (♛):** Eevee Bag (gold Item card)

### Wisdom of Sea and Sky — A4 (空と海の導き)

- **Released:** July 30, 2025 · **Cards:** 241 (161 base + 80 secret) · **Theme:** Johto (Gen II)
- **Booster packs (2):** Ho-Oh pack · Lugia pack
- **ex cards (10):** Ho-Oh ex, Lugia ex, Kingdra ex, Lanturn ex, Espeon ex, Donphan ex, Crobat ex, Umbreon ex, Skarmory ex, Shuckle ex
- **New mechanic:** **Baby Pokémon** — Basic Pokémon, 30 HP, no Weakness or Retreat cost

### Secluded Springs — A4a (未知なる水域)

- **Released:** August 28, 2025 · **Cards:** 105 (71 base + 34 secret) · **Theme:** Johto legendary beasts + Eon duo
- **Booster pack (1):** Secluded Springs
- **ex cards:** Entei ex, Suicune ex, Raikou ex, Jumpluff ex, Poliwrath ex
- **Star-rarity cameos:** Latias & Latios (regular and ☆)
- **Supporters:** Whitney, Morty, Traveling Merchant
- **Crown (♛):** Mantyke (#105)

### Deluxe Pack: ex — A4b (デラックスパックex)

- **Available:** Sept 30 – Oct 29, 2025 (re-run Jan 14–29, 2026) · **Cards:** 379 (353 + 26 secret)
- **Type:** First **Deluxe Pack** (analogous to a physical "High Class Pack") — a best-of compilation of every ◇◇◇◇ ex released in booster packs from Genetic Apex through Wisdom of Sea and Sky.
- **Mechanics:** packs contain **4 cards** (not 5); each guarantees at least one ◇◇◇◇-or-higher; introduced **parallel foil** cards; in Wonder Pick the 5th-card slot is replaced by two Pack Hourglasses.

## 2. B Series (2025 – 2026) — the Mega Evolution era

The B series launched on the game's first anniversary and introduced **Mega Evolution Pokémon ex** — a new card class worth **3 points** when Knocked Out (vs. 2 for a regular ex). Mega ex still use the ◇◇◇◇ / ☆ / ♛ rarity symbols. In Pocket, a Stage-2 Mega evolves directly from its Stage-1 form via normal evolution rules.

### Mega Rising — B1 (メガライジング)

- **Released:** October 30, 2025 (1st anniversary) · **Cards:** 331 (226 + 105 secret) · **Theme:** Mega Evolution debut
- **Booster packs (3):** Mega Gyarados · Mega Blaziken · Mega Altaria
- **Mega Evolution ex:** Mega Pinsir ex, Mega Blaziken ex, Mega Gyarados ex, Mega Ampharos ex, Mega Altaria ex, Mega Absol ex
- **Other ex:** Rapidash ex, Greninja ex, Jolteon ex, Indeedee ex, Hitmonchan ex, Dragalge ex, Melmetal ex
- **Crown (♛):** Lilligant, Klefki, Flame Patch (pullable from any of the three packs)

### Crimson Blaze — B1a (紅蓮ブレイズ)

- **Released:** December 17, 2025 · **Cards:** 103 (69 + 34 secret) · **Theme:** Kanto starter Megas + Kalos (Gen VI) Trainers
- **Booster pack (1):** Crimson Blaze
- **Mega Evolution ex (5):** Mega Venusaur ex, Mega Charizard Y ex, Mega Blastoise ex, Mega Lopunny ex, Mega Steelix ex
- **Legendary:** Xerneas · **Supporters:** Serena, Clemont
- **Crown (♛, 2):** Aegislash, Quick-Grow Extract

### Fantastical Parade — B2 (夢幻パレード)

- **Released:** January 29, 2026 · **Cards:** 234 (155 + 79 secret) · **first main expansion with a single booster pack**
- **New mechanic:** **Stadium cards** — Training Area, Starting Plains, Peculiar Plaza
- **Mega Evolution ex:** Mega Gardevoir ex, Mega Swampert ex, Mega Mawile ex, Mega Kangaskhan ex
- **Other ex:** Teal Mask Ogerpon ex, Blacephalon ex, Alolan Ninetales ex, Toxtricity ex, Mimikyu ex, Gigalith ex
- **Supporters:** Diantha, Sightseer, Juggler, Piers

### Paldean Wonders — B2a (パルデアワンダー)

- **Released:** February 26, 2026 · **Cards:** 131 · **Theme:** Paldea (Gen IX)
- **ex cards:** Meowscarada ex, Armarouge ex, Chien-Pao ex, Bellibolt ex, Gholdengo ex
- **Shiny ex reprints:** Entei / Suicune / Raikou
- **Supporters:** Iono, Nemona, Arven, Penny
- **Crown (♛, 2):** Baxcalibur, Electric Generator

### Mega Shine — B2b (シャイニングメガ)

- **Released:** March 26, 2026 · **Cards:** 117 · **Introduced Shiny Mega Evolution ex** (the B-series analog to Shining Revelry)
- **Mega Evolution ex (5, each with a shiny variant):** Mega Charizard X ex, Mega Slowbro ex, Mega Manectric ex, Mega Gengar ex, Mega Scizor ex
- **Shiny (✸, #87–115):** Scyther, the Charmander line, Pikachu/Raichu, Dratini, Haxorus
- **Supporters:** Iris, Calem · **Stadium:** Hiking Trail
- **Crown (♛, 2):** Arboliva, Metal Core Barrier

### Pulsing Aura — B3 (波動ビート)

- **Released:** April 28, 2026 · **Cards:** 234 (155 + 79 secret) · **Theme:** Mega Lucario / Fighting focus
- **Mega Evolution ex:** Mega Lucario ex, Mega Sceptile ex, Mega Camerupt ex, Mega Audino ex
- **Other ex:** Vaporeon ex, Magnezone ex, Crustle ex, Zoroark ex, Corviknight ex, Flygon ex
- **Support:** Korrina · **Stadium:** Arena of Antiquity

### Paradox Drive — B3a (進撃パラドックス)

- **Released:** May 28, 2026 · **Cards:** 109 · **Theme:** Ancient & Future (Paradox) Pokémon (Gen IX), centered on Koraidon / Miraidon
- **ex cards:** Iron Bundle ex, Miraidon ex, Flutter Mane ex, Koraidon ex, Terapagos ex
- **Mega Evolution ex:** Mega Altaria ex (Psychic)
- **Support:** Ancient/Future Booster Energy Capsule, Professor Sada, Professor Turo, Juliana · **Stadium:** Area Zero
- **Crown (♛, 2):** Iron Valiant, Roaring Moon

### Everyday Wonders — B3b (ミラクルデイズ)

- **Released:** June 30, 2026 · **Cards:** 106 (69 + 37 secret) · **Theme:** "everyday life"
- **ex cards:** Milotic ex, Dedenne ex, Hisuian Zoroark ex, Vaporeon ex
- **Mega Evolution ex:** Mega Diancie ex, Mega Sableye ex, Mega Ampharos ex, Mega Gyarados ex
- **Crown (♛, 2):** Munchlax, Small Balloon (Tool card)

### Ruler of the Skies — B4 (天空の支配者) (announced)

- **Scheduled:** July 30, 2026 · **Cards:** TBA · Next main expansion (headline: **Mega Rayquaza ex** is the widely expected chase card). Full details not yet released as of compilation.

## 3. Promo Sets

- **Promo-A (P-A):** from Oct 30, 2024, ~117 cards — event/login/shop promos for the A series.
- **Promo-B (P-B):** from Oct 30, 2025, ~70–78 cards — promos for the B series.

Promo cards are obtained from events, login bonuses, the shop (Shop/Premium Tickets), and bundles. **Promo cards cannot be traded.**
